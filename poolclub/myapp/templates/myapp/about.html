About Sansiro Pool Club

Welcome to Sansiro Pool Club, your favorite spot for an exciting and competitive pool billiard experience. Founded by lovers of the sport, our club offers a relaxed atmosphere, world-class pool tables, and a tight-knit community of members who share a passion for cue sports.

Whether you're a beginner or a pro, our club is designed to cater to all levels. We host regular tournaments, offer training sessions, and provide a perfect environment for socializing and learning.

Join us and be part of the Sansiro experience today.
